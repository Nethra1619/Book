First Day at College

On July 3, it was my first day at college. I quickly became close with Arthy, and she pointed out a senior in our class. "Look, doesn't he look like our senior?" she said. She dared me to go talk to him, and when I turned to look, there he was, making a grand entrance like a hero, tan tan tan taaaaan.

Initial Thoughts

At first, I wondered, "Where will I find friends to talk to?" Similarly, he probably thought, "Where will I find girls to talk to?" As days went by, we started a "friends forever" group, and the two of us joined and began talking. It was the beginning of an unexpected friendship.

Bonding and Growing Closer

Due to certain circumstances, we began to talk more and more, and now, we are best friends. Our friendship grew from strangers to brothers to friends to best friends. Now we are like family. I see him as a part of my family.
